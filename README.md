
# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard (Excel)

## 📌 Project Overview
This HR Analytics Dashboard is designed to provide actionable insights into workforce composition, salary distributions, recruitment trends, and employee demographics. It helps HR managers and stakeholders make data-driven decisions regarding payroll, hiring, and logistics.

---

## 🚀 Suggested Dashboard Layout & Visuals
To provide a comprehensive view, the dashboard includes:

*   **KPI Cards:** Total Employees, Total Basic Salary, Active Employees, Average Salary.
*   **Bar Chart:** Number of Employees by Department (sorted).
*   **Pie/Donut Chart:** Gender Distribution and Employee Type (Permanent vs. Probation).
*   **Bar Chart:** Average Salary by Designation.
*   **Line Chart:** Number of Joiners by Month (using Date of Join).
*   **Slicers (Interactive Filters):** Department, Working Status, Gender, and Employee Category.

---

## 📊 Key Metrics Analyzed

### ✅ Workforce Composition (Headcount)
*   **Total Headcount:** Total number of employees currently on the payroll.
*   **Departmental Split:** Employee distribution across Production, Quality, Packing, etc.
*   **Designation Breakdown:** Identifying roles with the highest number of employees (e.g., Line Operator, Loader).
*   **Employee Category:** Ratio of Management to Direct/Indirect workers.

### ✅ Salary & Financial Analysis
*   **Total Monthly Payroll:** Total "Basic Salary" expenditure per month.
*   **Average Salary by Department:** Identifying highest and lowest average basic salaries by department.
*   **Salary by Designation:** Comparison between "Line Operators" and other roles.
*   **Salary vs. Employee Type:** Difference in pay between "Permanent" and "Probation" employees.

### ✅ Recruitment & Retention
*   **Hiring Trends:** Time-series analysis showing monthly or quarterly joiners.
*   **Employee Status:** Percentage of "Active" vs. "Resigned" or "Fire" status.
*   **Confirmation Pipeline:** Tracking employees in their "Probation" period vs. "Permanent" status.

### ✅ Employee Demographics & Logistics
*   **Gender Diversity:** Male-to-female ratio across different departments.
*   **Commute Analysis:** Distance tracking (1–3km vs. 10–15km) for transport planning.
*   **Health & Records:** Distribution of Marital Status and Blood Groups for emergency planning.
